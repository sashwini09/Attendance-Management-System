# How to Add Variables in Railway

## Step 1: Go to Your Project
1. Open railway.app
2. Click on your project name

## Step 2: Find Variables
Look at your service card - you should see these options:
- **Deploy** (button)
- **Settings** (gear icon)
- **Variables** (sometimes showing a list)

## Step 3: Add SECRET_KEY
If you see "Variables" tab:
1. Click on it
2. Click "Add Variable"
3. Enter:
   - Key: `SECRET_KEY`
   - Value: `attendance-system-secret-2024`
4. Click Save

## Alternative: Settings Page
1. Click on your service
2. Look for **"Settings"** or **"Environment"**
3. Find **"Variables"** section
4. Add the SECRET_KEY

## If Using Free Tier Without Variables:
Railway's free tier may not show variables. In that case:
1. Go to your service settings
2. Look for "Environment" 
3. Add variables there
